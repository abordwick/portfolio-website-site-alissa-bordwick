My Work

Pluto Play – More Than Just An E-Wallet

Pluto Play is an e-wallet app that offers more than just your average e-wallet. It allows users to create budgets, set financial goals and organize reward/loyalty points. This project was created using the double diamond strategy (discover, define, develop and deliver). Throughout the process I used my research, IA, wireframing, prototyping and graphic design skills to come up with the end product.
